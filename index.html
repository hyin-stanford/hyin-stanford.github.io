Welcome to my web site!

I completed my PhD in Computational Mathematics at Stanford University, advised by Tze Leung Lai from the Department of Statistics. I have also been working closely with Johan Ugander from the MS&E Department and Austin Benson from the CS Department at Cornell. Before joining Stanford, I completed my undergraduate study at Fudan University in Shanghai, China, majoring in Mathematics.

My main research interests include network analysis, casual inference, unbiased modeling, and more generally, machine learning and data mining.
