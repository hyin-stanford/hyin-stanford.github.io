Research Interests

My research interests span a wide range within machine learning and data mining, including

  • network analysis: to develop concepts and algorithms that help understand and analyze structured data with complex relationship.
  • causal inference: to remove intrinsic bias and reduce variance in real-world data analysis and modeling with counterfactual reasoning.
  • general: modeling and optimization of machine learning problems and their analysis.
